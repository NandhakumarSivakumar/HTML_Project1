Chicken Biriyani

Chicken Biryani is a spiced mix of chicken and rice.

Incredients:

2 cups basmati rice, 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces), 2 onions (thinly sliced), 3 tomatoes (chopped), 4 cloves of garlic (minced), 1-inch piece of ginger (grated), 2 green chilies (slit lengthwise), 1/2 cup plain yogurt, biryani masala powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala powder (1-2 teaspoons each), a handful of chopped fresh coriander leaves, a handful of chopped fresh mint leaves, 4 cups of water, 4 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee, and salt to taste.

Procedure

            *In a deep pan, heat oil or ghee and sauté thinly sliced onions until golden brown. 
            *Remove half for garnishing. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. 
            *Sauté for a minute, then add chopped tomatoes and cook until softened.
            *Add the marinated chicken and cook until partially done. Boil basmati rice separately until 70-80% cooked. Drain the rice.
            *Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken in the pan. Sprinkle with chopped coriander and mint leaves. Drizzle oil or ghee on top.
            *Cover the pan and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and flavors meld.
            *Let the biryani rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fried onions.
